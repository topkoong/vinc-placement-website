About VINC Placement

Your Trusted Partner Since 1988

Established in Bangkok in 1988

Since 1988, VINC Placement has been Thailand's trusted partner for Japanese job placement. We specialize in preparing and placing Thai workers in Japan across all sectors - from blue-collar factory positions to specialized engineering roles. With over 10,000 successful placements and 35 years of experience, we understand both Thai workers' aspirations and Japanese employers' needs.

"Thai workers bring exceptional skill, dedication, and work ethic to Japanese workplaces. Our mission is to bridge cultures and create opportunities for Thai talent while delivering excellence to Japanese companies across all industries."

Over the past five years alone, VINC has successfully placed more than 10,000 Thai workers in Japanese companies across all sectors - from factory workers to engineering professionals - maintaining our commitment to excellence and cultural integration.
