About VINC Placement

Your Trusted Partner Since 1988

Established in Bangkok in 1988

Since 1988, VINC Placement has been Thailand's most trusted partner for Japanese job placement. We've successfully placed over 10,000 Thai workers in Japan - from factory floors to engineering firms. With 35+ years of experience, we understand both Thai workers' dreams and Japanese employers' standards.

"Thai workers bring exceptional dedication, skill, and reliability to Japanese workplaces. Our mission is simple: create life-changing opportunities for Thai talent while delivering excellence to Japanese companies."

In the past five years alone, we've placed 10,000+ Thai workers with Japanese employers across all industries - maintaining our 35-year reputation for quality, cultural understanding, and lasting success.
